I like to use Kindle when I'm learning a language. I can look up words I don't know immediately, as you can see below.

However as you can see below, the definitions are in Spanish which I'm not up to reading them just yet.

But if I scroll all the way down to the bottom, there is a translation function, as you can see below. There is also a speaker icon that you can click on to hear how the word is pronounced.

One of the many challenges of learning Spanish is there are so many idioms. How many idioms are there? Enough to make one swear. In the frame below, I have selected the part of a sentence I suspect contains an idiom. An option bar appears, and I click the “Lookup” option...
...and I am presented with a full translation of the phrase.

Because the phrase didn't make sense in the context of the rest of the sentence when I tried to translate it into English, I suspected that it was an idiom or an idiomatic phrase, so I asked ChatGPT, which confirmed that it was an idiomatic expression.
